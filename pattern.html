Theme: Beach Sunset

The theme for the website is a beach sunset. The sunset represents a natural instance of the concept of “zen” (i.e.: peaceful and calm). At the very top of the page, the sun is going to be featured in dark orange color (#FB8500). As the user scrolls down, the colors for the ocean (#023047), and sand (#FFB703), among other hues, will scroll by giving the impression of “retreating” from the beach.

The sun will remain stationary as the user scrolls, up to when the color for the beach is reached, giving the effect of “setting down”. At the very bottom, the tops of palm trees will scroll into picture.

The font should be sans-serif to make reading easier and incur in less eye strain. A first candidate would be “Dosis” as found on Google Fonts
